Polyunsaturated Fats


Polyunsaturated fats can help lower your LDL (bad) cholesterol.
Polyunsaturated fats include omega-3 and omega-6 fats.
These are essential fatty acids that the body needs for brain function and cell growth.
Our bodies DO NOT make essential fatty acids, so you can only get them from food.
