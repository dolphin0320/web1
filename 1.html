Monounsaturated fats

Monounsaturated fats are good for your health in several ways:
They can help lower your LDL (bad) cholesterol level.

Cholesterol is a soft, waxy substance that can cause clogged, or blocked, arteries (blood vessels). Keeping your LDL level low reduces your risk for heart disease and stroke. Monounsaturated fats help develop and maintain your cells.
